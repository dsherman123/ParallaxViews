About

Parallax Views, LLC is led by David S. Sherman, Ph.D.—entrepreneur, educator, licensed psychotherapist, and co-founder of Criteria Corp. David’s career bridges psychology, product design, and operational execution, with a focus on helping organizations make confident, evidence-based decisions.

Previously, David helped modernize AIMS Multimedia and pioneered DigitalCurriculum—an early streaming VOD service for education—before co-founding Criteria Corp the first SaaS pre-employment assessment platform. He continues to advise founders and build new products across software and media.

David previously served as Chief Operating Officer and Chief Financial Officer at Criteria Corp, until his retirement in 2024. David continues to sit on the Board of Directors. Under his leadership, Criteria grew into a global SaaS platform for talent assessment and development—delivering validated pre- and post-hire assessments, structured interviewing solutions, and collaboration tools. Today, Criteria serves more than 4,000 customers worldwide, with over 60 million assessments administered across 60+ countries in 30 languages.

Before Criteria, David was principal and co-CEO of AIMS Multimedia, where he spearheaded the company’s transformation from a 16mm film distributor into a digital leader in educational media. He designed interactive learning products and co-founded DigitalCurriculum, one of the first streaming video-on-demand platforms for education, which reached over 20 million students when AIMS was acquired by Discovery Communications.

Alongside his entrepreneurial work, David remains committed to psychology and the arts. He is a licensed psychotherapist and is currently completing a psychological-philosophical murder mystery novel, A Question of Balance
